Workflow cards

One card per pipeline step. Tag colour signals the role (trigger / action / output). Cards are dense by default — content rows expose the configured value, the footer carries timing + state. Failure and "needs setup" states surface inline rather than in a modal.

Replaces native <select> with a typed combobox: rich options (icon + title + meta), grouping, optional search, keyboard navigation (↑ ↓ Enter Esc), and click-outside-to-close. Anchors to the trigger; flips above when out of viewport.

Loading & empty states

Cards stay the same shape during fetch — content swaps for shimmering skeletons. Empty states use a single soft prompt instead of an illustration; cheaper to maintain and doesn't punch the user with whitespace.
